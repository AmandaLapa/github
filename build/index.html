About
the event

A free two-day single-track conference live on
September 15, 2022 in celebration of our culture and
Latinx heritage month.

You will join a live stream of interactive talks by industry experts in both Spanish, Portuguese, and English, with live captioning and translation.

Topics will include:
  • Software development
  • Professional development
  • Security
  • Best practices
  • Technical project management

Our
Speakers

We’re excited to bring over 100 speakers representing more than 20 countries to the agenda this year. With talks taking place in eight languages, there’s something for everyone. While you may see some familiar faces in the design world, we are especially proud that every single speaker this year is a first-time Figma conference speaker.
